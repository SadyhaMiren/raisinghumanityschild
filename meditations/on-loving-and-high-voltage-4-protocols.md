__on loving and high voltage: protocols__

high voltage is not pathology  
it is amplitude  


some systems operate at higher charge  
higher exposure  
higher consequence  


in such systems  
minor shifts register strongly  
small misalignments amplify  
latency matters  



high voltage without protocol  
tends toward escalation  


because resolution is high  
and reaction speed is fast  


protocol is not control  
protocol is structured latency  


it preserves charge  
without converting it into war  


in high-voltage systems  
exit must remain explicit.  


not implied  
not romanticized  
not threatened  


explicit exit  
reduces coercion.  


latency must be protected  
especially under activation.  


no immediate resolution  
no forced clarity  
no compression of time  


when activation rises  
interaction slows.  


weaponization is zero tolerance.  


knowledge is not leverage.  
vulnerability is not handle.  
intimacy is not authority.  


once conscious weaponization appears  
interaction pauses  


no argument continues  
past that threshold  


rupture is permitted  
escalation is not  


high voltage requires  
revisability under intensity.  


when positions cannot shift  
without loss of status  
militarization begins.  


power must not require demonstration  
strength must not require domination  


in high-voltage systems  
deterrence cannot be eroticized.  


conflict must not become stimulation  


if escalation produces charge  
the field is unstable.  


high voltage can remain alive  
only if  

– latency exceeds reflex  
– exit exceeds pride  
– exposure exceeds control  
– power exceeds the need to prove  


without protocol  
high voltage burns  


with protocol  
high voltage remains luminous.  


the difference  
is not feeling  
it is structure.
